Quantum Entanglement Communication

Securing the future of information exchange with entangled photon technology

US Patent #9,876,543 Published: 15 Jan 2025
← Back to Patents

Description

This patent introduces a cutting-edge method for secure quantum communication using entangled photon pairs. This method enables unhackable communication channels that are inherently secure by the laws of quantum physics, offering unprecedented security over traditional methods.

Innovation Highlights

  • Utilizes quantum entanglement for end-to-end encryption.
  • Zero-trust communication model with quantum key distribution (QKD).
  • Applicable for military, governmental, and financial sectors.

Technical Summary

The system generates entangled photon pairs distributed over long distances using fiber-optic or free-space links. The receiver devices perform quantum measurements that allow secure key generation immune to eavesdropping. Quantum no-cloning and measurement disturbance principles ensure tamper detection.
